This chart shows reported violations of state or municipal codes restricting the possession, sale, manufacture and/or cultivation of marijuana over time.

Industry Related Crimes are crimes related to the licensed marijuana industry and are committed against the industry or by the industry itself. An example is theft directly from a licensed marijuana retailer.

Non-Industry Related Crimes are crimes in which Marijuana is the primary target in the commission of these crimes but the marijuana has no readily apparent tie to a licensed operation. Examples include robbery committed under the guise of buying/selling marijuana and stolen marijuana from vehicles or residences.
